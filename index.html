About Me


Zhixin is now a Ph.D. student supervised by Prof. Ngo Chong-Wah, in Computer Science, Singapore Management University. His general research interests lie in the field of multimedia computing and human-in-the-loop interactive system. Now, he mainly focuses on interactive video retrieval, user simulation, and also, the amazing LLMs in the interactive retrieval scenario.
