About Me


MA Zhixin is currently a Research Scientist at Singapore Management University. He earned his Ph.D. in Computer Science from Singapore Management University in December 2024 and his Bachelor's degree from Shandong University in June 2019. His research interests span multimedia computing and human-in-the-loop interactive systems. His current work focuses on interactive video retrieval and cross-modal large language models.
